Key Geographic Findings:

  • North American markets (US & Canada) dominate performance, accounting for 65% of total conversions and maintaining above-average ROAS (4.2x and 3.9x respectively).
  • English-speaking markets show consistently higher conversion rates (4.8-5.0%) compared to non-English markets (4.2-4.5%).
  • European markets show higher CPCs but lower conversion rates, suggesting potential for localization improvements.
  • Australia demonstrates strong engagement with competitive CTR (3.24%) despite smaller volume.

Geographic Recommendations:

  • Increase budget allocation to US and Canadian markets given strong ROAS performance and scalability potential.
  • Implement localized ad copy and landing pages for European markets to improve conversion rates.
  • Test expanded targeting in Australia and consider increasing presence in similar markets (New Zealand).
  • Analyze timezone and dayparting strategies for international campaigns to optimize performance windows.
  • Consider currency fluctuation impacts on European market performance and adjust bidding strategies accordingly.
